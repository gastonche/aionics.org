Valued Proposition

The Aionics platform provides unique value to research teams

Platform automatically computes “fingerprints” of solids, liquids, and additives, and executes one-click training of machine learning models

Provides access to new machine learning models as they are published by the scientific community; customers can append their own data to re-train models

Models trained in the platform can be automatically applied to >200,000 candidate materials

The details of trained machine learning models are used to elucidate which chemical factors are driving performance the most, providing scientific insights to the user
